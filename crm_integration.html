The Process

How Your CRM Gets Built and Runs

We handle every step from setup to automation. You get a fully working system without touching a single line of code.

01
🔎

Discovery and Audit

We start by understanding your current process, where leads are coming from, how they are being handled, and where they are falling off. This gives us the blueprint for your system.

02
⚙

CRM Setup and Configuration

We build and configure your CRM from scratch: custom pipelines, contact fields, tags, deal stages, and user access. Everything is organized around how your business actually sells.

03
🔁

Lead Source Integration

We connect every lead source to your CRM: your website forms, landing pages, ad campaigns, social profiles, and inbound calls. Every lead flows in automatically, tagged and organized.

04
⚡

Automation Workflows Built

We build the follow-up sequences that trigger the moment a lead enters your system: instant texts, emails, voicemail drops, and task assignments so no lead waits more than 2 minutes for a response.

05
📅

Booking and Calendar Sync

Your CRM connects directly to your booking calendar. Leads can self-book from follow-up messages, and every appointment lands in your CRM with contact history, deal value, and notes attached.

06
📈

Launch, Track, and Optimize

We go live, monitor performance, and continuously optimize your pipeline stages, follow-up timing, and conversion points. Your CRM gets better the longer it runs.

What You Get

Every Feature Your
Pipeline Needs

A complete CRM setup built for client acquisition, not just contact storage.

👤

Unified Contact Records

Every lead, prospect, and client in one place with full conversation history, deal notes, tags, source tracking, and activity logs all attached to their profile.

📊

Visual Pipeline Management

Drag-and-drop deal boards and list views that show exactly where every contact is in your sales process, with deal values and probability rolled up automatically.

⚡

Instant Automated Follow-Up

Triggers fire the second a lead enters your CRM. SMS, email, and voicemail sequences go out automatically so every lead gets a personal touchpoint within minutes.

📱

Two-Way SMS and Email

Send and receive text messages and emails directly from the CRM. Replies are logged automatically so your team has full context on every conversation.

📅

Booking and Calendar Integration

Your CRM links directly to your scheduling system. Booked appointments sync automatically with contact records, and reminders go out without any manual work.

🎯

Lead Source Attribution

Know exactly which ad, form, campaign, or referral source every contact came from. Track cost per lead and cost per acquisition across all your marketing channels.

📈

Real-Time Reporting

Live dashboards showing pipeline value, conversion rates by stage, response times, team activity, and monthly revenue. All updated automatically in real time.

🔁

Third-Party Integrations

We connect your CRM to your existing tools: Facebook Ads, Google Ads, Make.com, Zapier, Stripe, QuickBooks, Calendly, and dozens of others depending on your stack.

🔒

Role-Based Team Access

Set custom permissions for sales reps, managers, and admins. Each team member sees only what they need, and you keep full visibility and control over the entire pipeline.
