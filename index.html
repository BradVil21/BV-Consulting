Common questions

Frequently asked

Do you offer remote or in-person support?

Both. Many issues, email, software, and tune-ups, we can solve remotely. For hardware, PC building, and equipment setup, we come to you or you drop the device off.

How much do you charge?

Every job is different, so we give you a clear quote up front with no hidden fees. Use our quick quote form and we'll respond, usually within 24 hours.

What if you can't fix my problem?

No fix, no fee. If we can't solve the issue, you don't pay for the labor, we'll only ever charge for parts you've approved.

Do you work with both homes and businesses?

Yes. We help families, individuals, and small businesses with everything from a single slow laptop to a full office equipment setup.

What areas do you serve?

We're based in Fort Lauderdale, FL and provide on-site tech support across Broward County and the wider South Florida area, including nearby cities. Remote support is available anywhere.
