About us

Technology, Made Simple

BV Consulting was founded on one belief: technology should work for you, not against you. We're the friendly experts you call when your computer won't cooperate, your new equipment won't connect, or your email just won't behave.

Our story

Built to take the stress out of tech

Most people don't have time to troubleshoot a frozen laptop, untangle a printer setup, or figure out why their email stopped syncing. That's where we come in. Based in Fort Lauderdale, FL, BV Consulting started as a way to give individuals and small businesses across South Florida honest, jargon-free tech help they could actually trust.

Today we handle everything from same-day computer repairs to custom PC builds and full office equipment installs. No problem is too small, and we explain every fix in plain English so you always know what's going on.
