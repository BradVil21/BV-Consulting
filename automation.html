What Gets Automated

Every Repetitive Task
Removed From Your Plate

If your team does it more than once the same way, we automate it. Here is what our backend workflows take over completely.

⚡

Instant Lead Response

The moment a lead fills out any form, submits a chat, calls your number, or clicks an ad, a personalized response fires automatically within 90 seconds. No one has to monitor an inbox or pick up the phone first.

📱

Multi-Touch Follow-Up Sequences

SMS, email, and voicemail drop sequences that run on a custom schedule for days or weeks after a lead comes in. Each message is tailored to where the prospect is in the journey, not a generic blast.

📅

Booking and Appointment Management

Confirmations, reminders at 24 hours and 1 hour out, post-call summaries, and reschedule links all sent automatically. Every appointment has a full communication trail without any manual sending.

👤

CRM Data Entry and Updates

Every contact, conversation, tag, note, and pipeline stage change updates automatically based on what actions are taken. No one on your team has to manually move a deal or log an activity again.

🔔

Internal Team Notifications

The right person gets notified at the right moment. Hot lead comes in? Sales rep is pinged instantly. Deal closes? Finance and delivery team get the handoff automatically. No one falls through the cracks.

🔁

Re-Engagement and Nurture

Leads who do not book immediately get enrolled in a long-term nurture sequence. Monthly touches, value content, and seasonal offers run automatically for months without anyone having to remember to follow up.

⭐

Review and Referral Requests

Google review requests and referral offers sent at the exact right moment in the client lifecycle, when satisfaction is highest. Tracked automatically so you know who reviewed and who referred.

💲

Invoice and Payment Triggers

Payment reminders, receipt confirmations, and overdue notices all sent automatically on your schedule. Connect with Stripe or QuickBooks and your billing communication runs on autopilot.
