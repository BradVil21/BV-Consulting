The 6-Phase Process

Everything We Do,
In Order

Each phase builds on the last. By the time we are done, you have a complete, automated client acquisition system running in the background while you focus on serving customers.

1
Phase 1  |  Week 1

Strategy Call and Onboarding

We start with a focused strategy call to understand your business, your target customer, the areas you serve, and what your current process looks like for handling leads. This shapes every decision we make. You will complete a short onboarding intake, and we will map out your full system before a single ad goes live or a workflow gets built.

🎯
Target Customer
We identify exactly who you want to reach, what they search for, and what makes them decide to buy.
📍
Service Area
We map your geographic targeting so ads only show to people in the areas you actually serve.
📄
System Blueprint
We create a complete roadmap of your ads, CRM, automations, and booking flow before we build anything.
2
Phase 2  |  Week 1 to 2

Google Search Ads Build and Launch

We research the exact keywords your best prospects use when searching for your service, write compelling ads, build a high-converting landing page, and set up conversion tracking. Every campaign is structured to maximize leads at the lowest possible cost per acquisition.

🔍
Keyword Research
We find high-intent search terms that signal a prospect is ready to hire, not just browsing.
✍️
Ad Copywriting
Compelling headlines and descriptions written to earn clicks from your ideal customer.
📈
Tracking Setup
Full conversion tracking so we know exactly which ads, keywords, and placements are generating real leads.
3
Phase 3  |  Week 2

CRM Build and Contact Pipeline

We build your CRM and organize every lead into a clear pipeline so nothing falls through the cracks. Every lead from your Google Ads, website, or any other source flows directly into your CRM automatically. You always know exactly where each prospect stands.

👥
Lead Pipeline
Custom pipeline stages built around your sales process from new lead to closed client.
🔁
Auto-Import
Every lead from every source lands in your CRM automatically, no manual data entry required.
📲
Contact Records
Full contact profiles with lead source, conversation history, notes, and deal value all in one place.
4
Phase 4  |  Week 2 to 3

Automation and Follow-Up Workflows

This is where most businesses lose leads and where we make sure you never do. We build automated sequences that respond to new leads within seconds, follow up by text and email over days and weeks, and move prospects through your pipeline without you lifting a finger.

⚡
Instant Response
New leads receive a personalized text within 60 seconds of submitting a form, 24 hours a day.
💌
Multi-Touch Follow-Up
SMS, email, and voicemail sequences that nurture leads who did not book right away.
🕐
Smart Timing
Follow-up messages sent at the right times based on lead behavior and time of day for maximum response rates.
5
Phase 5  |  Week 3

Booking System and Calendar Integration

We set up your booking system so leads can schedule appointments directly without any back-and-forth. Automated confirmation texts and email reminders go out before every appointment, and no-shows drop dramatically. Your calendar stays full with qualified, confirmed bookings.

📅
Online Booking
Leads can book an appointment directly from your site or through a follow-up link, any time of day.
🔔
Reminders
Automated appointment confirmation and reminder texts that cut no-show rates significantly.
🔗
Calendar Sync
Your booking system syncs directly with your calendar so you never get double-booked.
6
Phase 6  |  Ongoing

Optimization, Reporting, and Ongoing Management

Once everything is live, we do not disappear. We monitor your campaigns, test new ad variations, adjust bids, refine targeting, and send you regular performance reports. Your system gets smarter over time and your cost per lead goes down as we gather more data.

📊
Monthly Reports
Clear, plain-English performance reports covering leads, cost per lead, bookings, and campaign health.
🧰
Continuous Testing
We are always testing new headlines, keywords, and landing page elements to improve performance.
📞
Ongoing Support
Direct access to our team whenever you have questions, need adjustments, or want to expand.

Phase 4

Follow-Up That
Never Sleeps

The average business takes over 24 hours to respond to a new lead. We build automations that respond in under 60 seconds, follow up for weeks, and never let a lead go cold without a fight.

1
Instant Lead Response A personalized text message is sent to every new lead within 60 seconds, day or night, no manual action needed.
2
Multi-Channel Sequences Leads are followed up via SMS, email, and voicemail drops across a sequence that runs for days or weeks until they respond.
3
Behavior-Based Branching Workflows adjust based on what a lead does. Booked an appointment? They move to a reminder sequence. No response? They stay in the follow-up loop.
4
Team Notifications Your team gets notified the moment a hot lead responds so you can step in and close while the iron is hot.
